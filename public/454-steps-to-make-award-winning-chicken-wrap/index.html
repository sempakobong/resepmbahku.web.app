The ingredients needed to make Chicken Wrap:
  1. Get 1 Chicken leg (boneless)
  2. Take 8 Leaves lettuce
  3. Take 1 Ripe and firm tomato
  4. Prepare Siracha hot sauce
  5. Prepare 2 Soft con tortillas
  6. Make ready Spices
  7. Get 1 Teaspoon paprika
  8. Prepare 1 Teaspoon garlic powder
  9. Prepare 1 Teaspoon salt
  10. Take 1 Teaspoon pepper
  11. Make ready 2 Tablespooons of oil
  12. Make ready Utensils
  13. Get Cutting board
  14. Get Knife
  15. Get Grilling pan
  16. Make ready Tongs
  17. Get Plate
  18. Make ready Big bowl (that you can close\ has a lid)
  19. Prepare Small bowl
  20. Take Mesuring spoons
Instructions to make Chicken Wrap:
  1. In a small bowl mix all of the spices anad add the oil to create a sauce, then in the big bowl put the chicken and pour the sauce, cover with thelid and let sit.
  2. While the chicken is sitting wash the lettuce and the tomatoes, then cut the lettuce into small pieces and the tomato into thin rounds.
  3. Take the chicken that has ben siting in the marinade and cook for 5 to 7 minutes on the grillig pan then let sit for 3 minutes
  4. After the three minutes pass cut the chicken into small cubes.
  5. Take a tortilla and set it on the plate, then to aswemle the wrap place the tomatoes first then add the letuche then the chicken and at the end add the siracha (make sure yoou do this centered or else it wont close). Fold the sides wraping the filling and at last fold the edges inwards to close.
  6. Cut the wrap in half (optional) and ENJOY !!!!
