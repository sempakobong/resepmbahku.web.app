  1. Make ready For the chilli chicken
  2. Prepare Chicken (boneless)
  3. Take 1/2 cup corn flour
  4. Take 1 egg
  5. Prepare 1 onion diced
  6. Prepare 1/2 red/yellow bell peppers

  8. Prepare 1 tbsp chilli sauce
  9. Get 1 tbsp stir fry sauce
  10. Take For the noodles
  11. Make ready 1 packet noodles (chings hakka)
  12. Get Peppercorns
  13. Take 1 egg (scrambled)

  15. Get Salt
