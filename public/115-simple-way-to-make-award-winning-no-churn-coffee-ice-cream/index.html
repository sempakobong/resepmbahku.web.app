The ingredients needed to make No Churn Coffee Ice Cream:
  1. Take 1/4 cup coffee bean, coarse grind
  2. Get 1 can sweetened condensed milk
  3. Prepare 2 cups whipping cream, cold
  4. Take 2 tbs liquor, optional
Steps to make No Churn Coffee Ice Cream:
  1. Combine whipping cream and coffee bean, cover and let it steep for 12 hours or overnight in the refrigerator.
  2. Strain the mixture to your mixing bowl, whisk it until soft peak. Do not overbeat.
  3. Mix in the milk and liquor if using, stir well.
  4. Transfer to container, freeze for a few hours or until frozen.
