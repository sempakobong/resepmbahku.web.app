The ingredients needed to make Apple pie with crockan ice cream:
  1. Prepare 2 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  2. Get 200 grams unsalted butter
  3. Get 2 tbsp milk
  4. Take 1/2 tsp lemon zest
  5. Get 1 tsp sugar
  6. Prepare pinch salt
  7. Prepare 3 large apple
  8. Make ready 1 tsp cinnamon
  9. Take 3 tbsp brown sugar
  10. Prepare pinch ground nutmeg
  11. Prepare 2 tbsp cornstarch
  12. Make ready 1 tbsp lemon juice
