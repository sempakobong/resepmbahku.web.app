The ingredients needed to make Apple Crumble with Ice-cream:
  1. Make ready 1 Red apples
  2. Take 1 Green apples or Granny Smith apples
  3. Get 1/3 cup sugar
  4. Take 1 Lemon
  5. Get 1 tbsp Cinnamon ground
  6. Prepare 1/2 cup Flour
  7. Prepare 1/2 cup Soften butter ( not melt )
  8. Take 1 tbsp Salt and pepper
  9. Prepare 2 tbsp Whipped cream
  10. Make ready 1 Ice -cream scoop
  11. Make ready 1 Vanila bean
  12. Prepare 1/3 cup Dried cranberries
  13. Make ready 1/3 cup Brown sugar
  14. Prepare 1 cup Museli
Instructions to make Apple Crumble with Ice-cream:
  1. Wash and cut red apples into very small pieces.
  2. Caramelize sugar and add some Vanila seed from the bean , pinch of cinnamon
  3. Add red apples then chop green apples roughly and add well to the pot , add lemon zest and lemon juice. Add some dried cranberries on top.
  4. Remove from heat and take a rest for half an hour.
  5. Add flour , butter , brown sugar and cinnamon into the food processor , mix with muesli and bring them top of the apple layer
  6. Turn the gas back on and heat until bubbling
  7. Bake at 200¬∞F c for 13 minutes
