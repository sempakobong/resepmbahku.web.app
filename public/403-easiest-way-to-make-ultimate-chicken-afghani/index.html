The ingredients needed to make Chicken Afghani:
  1. Prepare 1 kg chicken
  2. Make ready 20 cashews
  3. Take 1 cup milk
  4. Make ready 3 big onions thinly sliced
  5. Prepare 1 tomato pur√©ed
  6. Make ready 1 tbsp Aamchur powder
  7. Make ready 1 tbsp garam masala powder
  8. Take 1 tbsp red chilli powder
  9. Take 1 tsp coriander powder
  10. Make ready 1 tsp ginger garlic paste
  11. Prepare 1 tbsp dried Kasuri methi
  12. Make ready 2 tbsp chopped coriander leaves
  13. Get 3 tbsp refined oil
  14. Take 3 tbsp ghee
  15. Take To Marinate:
  16. Take 1/2 lemon juice
  17. Prepare 2 tbsp curd
  18. Prepare 2 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  19. Take 1 tbsp red chilli powder
  20. Take 1 tbsp salt
Steps to make Chicken Afghani:
  1. Marinate the chicken with the ingredients listed in marinade section and refrigerate for an hour.
  2. Add 3 tbsp of oil In a pan and shallow fry the chicken. Cook for 20 minutes by covering the pan with a lid. Keep the cooked chucked aside.
  3. In the same pan, fry the onions and cashew till they are golden brown. Cool for some time and grind them in a mixer to make a smooth paste.
  4. Add ghee to a pan and fry the ginger garlic paste for 5 minutes. Add tomato pur√©e, amchur powder, garam masala powder, coriander powder, salt and milk and cook for 10 minutes.
  5. Add the onion cashew paste and kasuri methi and cook for another 5 minutes.
  6. Add the chicken pieces and coriander leaves and stir well.
  7. Enjoy it hot with rice/roti!
