The ingredients needed to make Brownie Cookie Ice Cream Sandwiches:
  1. Get 16 oz semi sweet chocolate, chopped
  2. Prepare 4 tbsp unsalted butter
  3. Take 4 large eggs
  4. Take 1 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  5. Get 1 tsp vanilla extact
  6. Make ready 1/2 tsp baking powder
  7. Make ready 1/4 tsp salt
  8. Get 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  9. Prepare 6 oz semi sweet chocolate chips
  10. Make ready 1 tsp vanilla extract
  11. Take 2 quart vanilla ice cream
  12. Make ready 2 cup toasted coconut, recipe below
  13. Prepare 1 1/2 cup sprinkles

Instructions to make Brownie Cookie Ice Cream Sandwiches:
  1. In a double boiler or a heat proof bowl set over a saucepan of simmering water, stirring a few times, melt the chocolate and butter just until smooth., remove from heat, set aside.
  2. In a large bowl beat eggs, salt and sugar until thick and pale, about 3 to 5 minutes.Beat in vanilla extract and salt. Fold in melted chocolate/butter mixture.. Fold in flour and baking powder, then the chocolate chips.
  3. Cover dough and refrigerate until cold, about 2 hours.
  4. Preheat oven to 350. Line baking sheets with parchment paper.
  5. Working in batches, scoop 2 tablespoon mounds of dough and place two inches apart on baking sheet, bake about 10 minutes until cookies are dry around edges and cracked on top. Cool on cookie sheet 10 minutes before moving to a baking rack to cool completely before making ice cream sandwiches.
  6. To make ice cream sandwiches, put some vanilla ice cream on bottom of one cookie, top with another cookie and press lightly to make a sandwich. Roll edges in toasted coconut for half of sandwiches and sprinkles for the other half. - - https://cookpad.com/us/recipes/358497-toasted-coconut
  7. Other options for rolling edges are chopped nuts, mini m&m's, or mini chocolate chips. They can be left plain as well!
  8. Wrap each finished ice cream in plastic wrap, keep frozen.
