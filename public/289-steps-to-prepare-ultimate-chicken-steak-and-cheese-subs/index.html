The ingredients needed to make Chicken steak and cheese subs:
  1. Prepare 2 pounds ground chicken
  2. Take 1 bell pepper diced
  3. Prepare 1 medium onion diced
  4. Make ready 1 Tsp onion powder
  5. Get 1 tsp garlic powder
  6. Make ready 1 and a half tsp adobo
  7. Get 1 tsp black pepper
  8. Get Dash salt
  9. Prepare 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  10. Take 6 subrolls

Instructions to make Chicken steak and cheese subs:
  1. Cook ground chicken in medium skillet
  2. Add diced peppers and onions
  3. Cook for another five to seven minutes until onions and peppers are tender.
  4. Place cheese on the top of ground chicken cover and let melt. Place chicken in sub rolls and enjoy…
