  5. Get 1/2 tsp Clarified Butter/Ghee

  9. Get 1 tsp Sugar

Steps to make Chikki IceCream:
  1. Heat a heavy bottom non stick flat bottom pan add sesame seeds into it and dry roast them
  2. Take them out in a plate
  3. Add Jaggery into the same pan and let it melt on low flame
  4. When it becomes fluffy and light
  5. Add Roasted sesame seeds into it. mix them properly and switch off the gas
  6. Grease the kitchen platform and rolling pin with ghee
  7. Take this mixture out quickly on kitchen platform in two portions
  8. Roll them out thinly
  9. Make a cone out of it, do it very quickly
  10. Put this cone in a glass so that it will be in the cone shape
  11. Heat a tadka pan
  12. Add Clarified Butter and chopped almonds into it
  13. Roast them for a while
  14. Add Sugar and mix
  15. Add choco chips and switch off the gas and let it cool down
  16. Stuffing is ready. Pour it into the cone
  17. Refrigerate them for 30 minutes
  18. Chikki is ready to relish
