The ingredients needed to make condense milk icecream:

  2. Make ready 60 ml caster sugar
  3. Make ready 150 ml milk
  4. Take 150 ml cream
  5. Take 100 ml condense milk
  6. Take 5 ml vanilla essence

Steps to make condense milk icecream:
  1. Beat egg yolks and caster sugar together until egg yolks are light yellow
  2. Bring milk to the boil in a pot on the stove top
  3. Once milk is boiling remove from heat and pour into egg mixture whilst beating
  4. Pour mixture back into pot and and cook over low heat until mixture has thickened
  5. Take off stove and leave to cool down
  6. Once cool add cream, condense milk and vanilla essence.
  7. Transfer into a container suitable for freezing and place in the freezer for around five hours
  8. Makes around 600ml
