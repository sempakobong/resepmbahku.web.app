The ingredients needed to make Vegan Banana & Chocolate Ice Cream:
  1. Prepare 3 frozen bananas
  2. Get 2 1/2 tablespoons cacao powder
  3. Take 1/4 tsp Herbilogy's habatussauda powder
  4. Get Cacao nibs
  5. Prepare Almond Butter Cookie Crumble
Instructions to make Vegan Banana & Chocolate Ice Cream:
  1. Blend frozen banana, cacao powder and habatussauda powder until creamy
