The ingredients needed to make Chicken Spinach Bahaar / Hariyali Chicken:
  1. Get 1 kg Chicken
  2. Get 1 bunch Spinach
  3. Prepare Mint leaves- few
  4. Get Coriander leaves- few
  5. Get 5 Tbsp Curd
  6. Take 1 Tbsp Garlic paste
  7. Prepare 1 Tbsp Ginger paste
  8. Prepare Green chilli chopped- 1 nos
  9. Get 1 Tbsp Red chilli powder
  10. Take 1/2 tsp Garam Masala powder
  11. Make ready 1 tsp Cumin powder
  12. Take 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder
  13. Take 1 tsp Coriander powder
  14. Take 2 Tbsp Lime juice
  15. Take Whole spice blend:-
  16. Take 1/2 Tbsp Black pepper whole
  17. Prepare 1 Tbsp Cumin seeds
  18. Take 2 pc Cloves
  19. Prepare 2 pc Green cardamom
  20. Prepare 1 inchCinnamon stick
  21. Take 1 Tbsp Fennel seeds-
  22. Make ready 1 Tbsp Coriander seeds-
  23. Take For Gravy:-
  24. Prepare Onion medium size- 2 (chopped)
  25. Get Tomato -1 cup (blended)
  26. Make ready 1 Tbsp Ginger garlic paste
  27. Get 2 Tbsp Ghee
  28. Make ready to taste Salt
Steps to make Chicken Spinach Bahaar / Hariyali Chicken:
  1. Add blanched spinach in a blender with mint & coriander leaves. Add lime juice, ginger garlic paste, green chilli, curd & add all spices. Blend it to a puree.
  2. Puree is ready. Marinate chicken & keep it aside for 30 mins.
  3. Roast whole spices & blend it.
  4. Heat a heavy bottom pan add. 2 Tbsp of ghee. Add chopped onion & cook till it becomes soft & translucent.
  5. Add ginger garlic paste & let it cook for about 2-3 mins so that the raw smell goes away.
  6. Add whole spice blended masala. Now time to add marinated chicken in the pan. Mix it well. Cook for 5-8 mins.
  7. Add fresh tomato puree & salt. Cover the lid & let it cook for 8-10 min. Chicken Spinach Bahar is ready. Enjoy ðŸ˜Š
