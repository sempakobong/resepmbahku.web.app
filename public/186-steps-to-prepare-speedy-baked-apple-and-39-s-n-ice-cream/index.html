The ingredients needed to make Baked Apple's n Ice Cream:
  1. Prepare 6 apples
  2. Get 6 tsp butter
  3. Make ready 6 dried dates
  4. Take 1 vanilla sugar
  5. Prepare 1 chai spiced sugar
  6. Prepare 1 Hokey Pokey ice cream (or favourite flavour)
Steps to make Baked Apple's n Ice Cream:
  1. Preheat oven to 180°F
  2. Core apples
  3. Using a sharp knife cut all the way around the skin of the apples (half way down)
  4. Stand apples in a baking dish
  5. Place 1 tsp each of vanilla sugar and Thai spiced sugar into each apple (you can use any flavored sugar or simply use brown sugar)
  6. Place a tsp of butter into each apple and press a dried date into it
  7. Pour enough water into dish to cover the base
  8. Bake in oven at 180°F for around 25 minutes or until apples are golden n soft
  9. Serve with Hokey Pokey ice cream (or ice cream of choice)
