The ingredients needed to make Kitkat walled Cookies and Cream Ice Cream Cake:
  1. Get cake
  2. Get 1 box devils food cake
  3. Get 1500 ml Cookies and Cream ice cream
  4. Make ready 1 packages oreo cookies
  5. Make ready 3 packages kitkat chocolate
Steps to make Kitkat walled Cookies and Cream Ice Cream Cake:
  1. Prepare the cake mixture according to package instruction
  2. Bake cake into two pans 9in dia. and keep it cool for 20 mins. after baking
  3. Layer the cake. 1st the one pan of cake then add 2 scoops of ice cream
  4. Then add half of the oreo cookies (cracked)
  5. Add another layer of ice cream
  6. Add the 2nd cake on top and add the remaining ice cream
  7. Toppwd with the half pack of oreo cookies
  8. Freeze overnight and add the kitkat walling
