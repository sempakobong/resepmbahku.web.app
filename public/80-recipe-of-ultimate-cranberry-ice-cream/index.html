The ingredients needed to make Cranberry Ice Cream:
  1. Get 10 oz Cranberry Puree, see my note above
  2. Get 1 1/2 cups Heavy Cream
  3. Prepare 1 1/2 Cups Regular Milk
  4. Take 1 cup sugar
  5. Prepare 1-2 tbsp Lemon Juice
Steps to make Cranberry Ice Cream:
  1. Blend together all of the ingredients together in a blender or a use a hand mixer. It is cool if there are still pieces of cranberry in the ice cream mixture.
  2. Let sit in the refrigerator for 6 hours or overnight.
  3. Turn on the ice cream maker machine, add the ice cream mixture, and follow the directions of your ice cream maker.
  4. When done, place it into the freezer and let it freeze for at least 2 hours in an airtight container.
  5. Enjoy!
