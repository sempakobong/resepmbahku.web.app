The ingredients needed to make Doggy "Ice Cream":
  1. Get 32 oz Plain Yogurt
  2. Make ready 1 cup Peanut Butter
  3. Take 3 Ripe Bananas
  4. Take 1 Ice Cube Tray(s)
  5. Take 1 Blender
Instructions to make Doggy "Ice Cream":
  1. Measure out yogurt and peanut butter. Break up bananas, and add all to your blender.
  2. Blend ingredients until they are super smooth.
  3. Pour mixture into ice cube tray(s).
  4. Put in freezer for at least 6 hours or overnight.
  5. Once frozen, "crack" tray to loosen, and give treat to your furry friend. (Some cats like them too)
