The ingredients needed to make Banana Ice Cream:
  1. Prepare 2 Bananas
  2. Make ready 1 blender or food processor
Instructions to make Banana Ice Cream:
  1. Peel and slice Bananas.
  2. Place Bananas on cookie sheet or wax paper, freeze.
  3. Put frozen Bananas in blender and blend.
  4. Push Bananas down from side and re blend until nice and creamy.
