The ingredients needed to make Mint chocolate chip ice cream:
  1. Make ready ice cream
  2. Prepare 14 oz Can of sweetened condensed milk
  3. Prepare 1 cup Heavy whipping cream
  4. Take 1 cup (or more) Chocolate of your choice
  5. Take 1 tbsp - 1 1/2 tbs of peppermint extract

For a mint ice cream, you can go the route of steeping real mint leaves in the cream, or you can use mint extract. Instructions to make Mint chocolate chip ice cream:
  1. First chill your condensed milk, heavy whipping cream, whisk attachment, and bowl.
  2. Beat your whipping cream until soft peaks. Beat for another minute and add in your condensed milk and peppermint extract.
  3. Finally chop your chocolate and add into the ice cream.
  4. Freeze for at least 3 hours before serving.
