The ingredients needed to make Balsamic Grilled Chicken & Tomatoes:
  1. Make ready 2 Grilled Chicken Breasts
  2. Prepare 1 Tomato
  3. Prepare 1/2 Head Green Leaf Lettuce
  4. Prepare 1/2 Onion
  5. Make ready 2 cloves Garlic
  6. Make ready Black Pepper
  7. Take Salt
  8. Prepare Italian Seasonings
  9. Prepare 1/4 Cup Balsamic Vinegar
  10. Make ready Handful Crackers (Your Choice)
Steps to make Balsamic Grilled Chicken & Tomatoes:
  1. Prep meat and veggies while heating some olive oil in a pan.
  2. Sauté onion and garlic for a couple minutes. Add in chicken and sauté a couple more minutes. Add in the tomato. (If you’re making this, I am assuming that you’ve already seasoned your chicken while grilling. Go light on the seasonings. The chicken will carry some flavor). Lightly season with salt, pepper and Italian seasonings. Add balsamic vinegar and continue cooking a couple minutes.
  3. Lastly, add in the lettuce. Break up some crackers in a bag (or in your hand) to top with later.
  4. Plate and serve.
