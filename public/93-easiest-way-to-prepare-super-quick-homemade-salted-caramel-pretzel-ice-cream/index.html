Easiest Way to Prepare Super Quick Homemade Salted Caramel Pretzel Ice Cream

To get started with this particular recipe, we have to first prepare a few ingredients. You can cook salted caramel pretzel ice cream using 4 ingredients and 6 steps. Here is how you can achieve that.

The ingredients needed to make Salted Caramel Pretzel Ice Cream:
  1. Take 3.5 cups Ice Cream Base (http://www.seriouseats.com/recipes/2015/04/dense-rich-chewy-new-england-ice-cream-recipe.html)
  2. Get 100 g semi-sweet chocolate
  3. Make ready 50 g Salted Caramel (https://www.twopeasandtheirpod.com/salted-caramel-sauce/)
  4. Get 25 g Pretzel sticks chopped/broken into tiny pieces
Steps to make Salted Caramel Pretzel Ice Cream:
  1. Freeze the chopped pretzels.
  2. Melt the chocolate and caramel, but keep them as cool as possible without going hard. If they are too hot they will melt the ice-cream when added. You can add 1/2 tsp canola oil to the chocolate to keep it liquid at lower temperatures
  3. Churn the ice cream as per instructions on your ice cream maker
  4. When there is only 2-3 minutes left to churn, add the pretzels and then drizzle the chocolate in thin stream and then drizzle the caramel in thin stream. The pretzels should be well distributed and the chocolate & caramel should freeze in thin veins throughout.
  5. Immediately transfer to a pre-chilled ice cream container and freeze for 4-6 hours.
  6. Enjoy!
