The ingredients needed to make Dill Pickle Chicken Salad:
  1. Prepare 3 C. Cooked and shredded chicken
  2. Prepare 1 C. Dill pickles sliced
  3. Take 1/2 Red onion, finely chopped
  4. Prepare 2 Celery stalks, finely chopped
  5. Take 2/3 C. Mayonnaise
  6. Take 1/4 C. Dijon mustard
  7. Get 2 Tbsp. Red wine vinegar
  8. Prepare 1 Tbsp. Chopped dill
  9. Make ready 1/2 tsp. Red pepper flakes
  10. Get to taste Salt and pepper
  11. Prepare 3 pitas, split and toasted
Instructions to make Dill Pickle Chicken Salad:
  1. Preheat oven to 400¬∞F. In a large bowl combine the shredded chicken, pickles, red onion, and celery.
  2. In another bowl stir together mayonnaise, Dijon mustard, red wine, vinegar, and dill. Season with red pepper flakes, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour dressing over chicken and tools to coat.
  4. Place split pitas on a baking sheet, drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with salt. Bake in oven 5 minutes until lightly browned and crispy. Serve with chicken salad.
