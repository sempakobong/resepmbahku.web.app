The ingredients needed to make Grilled lemon pepper chicken sandwich:
  1. Make ready 1 chicken breast
  2. Get Lemon pepper seasoning
  3. Take 1 tablespoon butter
  4. Make ready Onion powder
  5. Prepare 2 buns
  6. Take Mayo
  7. Prepare Shredded lettuce
  8. Take Slices tomatoes
  9. Make ready Slices dill pickles
  10. Take 2 pieces Swiss cheese

Instructions to make Grilled lemon pepper chicken sandwich:
